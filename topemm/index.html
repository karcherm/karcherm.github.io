TOPEMM

TOPEMM is a EMS driver for TOPCAT based mainboards. TOPCAT is the marketing name of the VLSI VL82C286/VL82C386sx and VL82C386 chipsets. These chipsets use the VLSI VL82C320 or VLSI VL82C330 system controller. This chipset also exists with Intel labels: The VL82C286 chipset is also known as Intel 82340SX, the VL82C386 chipset is also know as Intel 82340DX. The system controller chips are known as Intel 82343 and 82346 respectively.

This driver is based on the LTEMM driver by Lo-Tech, but has been heavily modified.
